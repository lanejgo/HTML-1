My Favorite Foods

My favorite meal has to be pho. Nothing beats that broth. I am partial to Thai and Greek food, as well, and I love a good burger. Recently my favorite place to get a burger has been Shake Shack. My favorite meal that my mom makes is red curry with chicken, which I have had a lot but never seem to grow tired of. My favorite dessert is probably ice cream cake or pie of any variety.

Rankings

  1. Pho
  2. Curry
  3. Gyros

My Favorite Bands

To give you a straight answer on my favorite band of all time is difficult, but for the last two years my top artist has been Radiohead by a landslide, which I think accurately captures my frequent obsession with their music. This last year I gave their album Kid A another chance after not liking it the first time, but I am so glad that I did because it quickly became one of my favorite albums of all time. If I had to name other bands that have been crucial to my psychological development, I would be remiss to not mention Pink Floyd and my bloody valentine, which is spelled without capitalization so don't you dare think I spelled it like that by mistake. I would describe the latter's music as a blanket that covers you completely in sweet, sweet sound. Lately, the bands that I have been listening to the most are The Smiths, Muse, and The Velvet Underground. I just recently discovered Muse, which is crazy to me because their music fits perfectly within my taste. I like their most popular album, Black Holes and Revelations, but an earlier album from 2001 called Origin of Symmetry is also really good.

Rankings

  • Radiohead
  • my bloody valentine
  • Pink Floyd
